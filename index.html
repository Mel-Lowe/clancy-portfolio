Clancy Ryan

Director. Choreographer. Actor. Educator

About

Clancy is an actor and creative from Sydney and Singapore. She is a graduate of School of the Arts, Singapore and ArtsEd.
As an actor she has worked at some of the UK's most reputable theatres including the RSC and Chichester Festival Theatre. As a creative, Clancy has worked as a director, choreographer and movement director on immersive projects, regional productions and for leading drama schools.
She is passionate about the work she does with emerging artists. As a young person she was a member of Singapore Repertory Theatre's Young Company. She experienced first hand the power of the arts to build community and inspire creativity. To continue in this tradition, she has worked as a creative/ tutor at institutions such as, Royal Central School of Speech and Drama, ArtsEd, and Liverpool Media Academy. When running a rehearsal room, Clancy emphasises safe practice so that actors feel supported, and encourages a culture of play and open conversation.

Workshops

Clancy teaches private students in voice and acting technique, and offers workshops that cater to actors of all ages in the following: Acting Through Song, Audition Prep, Scene Study, Dance Technique and Dance Repertoire.
